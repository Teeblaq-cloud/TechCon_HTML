Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for software developers and has grown into a global platform for innovation, education, and industry collaboration. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of participants from all over the world.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering an inclusive, forward-thinking space where technology leaders, enthusiasts, and learners can collaborate, share ideas, and build the future together.
